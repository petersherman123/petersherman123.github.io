Peter Sherman

About Me

I am a third-year PhD student working with Michael McElroy in the Department of Earth and Planetary Sciences at Harvard University. I previously completed a joint bachelor-master's degree in physics at Imperial College London, U.K. My research interests are broad. During my PhD, I have focused specifically on integration of renewable technologies, interactions of external forcing and internal variability in the climate system and air quality. If you have interest in discussing work or collaborating, please feel free to contact me at petersherman@g.harvard.edu.
